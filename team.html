Team

  • Founder. PhD (University of Bergen) in Multiphase Systems and Physics, with extensive research and hands-on experience in cyclone technology, fluid dynamics, process technology, and multiphase flow-electric-mechanical system architecture.

Elsa (Yi-Wen) WU

  • Founding Team, COO. With expertise in climate policy and sustainable innovation, Elsa connects research with operations and strategy. She leads partnerships and execution to translate green technologies into measurable real-world impact.

Alex C. HOFFMANN

  • Advisor. Emeritus Professor, University of Bergen, world-renowned expert in multiphase cyclone separators, with over 269 publications and extensive experience in industrial and scientific innovation.

Yi-Chun CHEN

  • Advisor. PhD (University of Bergen) in Physics and Technology, MSc in Electrical Engineering, with expertise in sensor systems and the integration of electronic, mechanical, and structural technologies for underwater and environmental applications.
