What CHATTIGR AS Does

CHATTIGR AS delivers advanced solutions and services for sustainable water and energy infrastructure, focusing on the critical challenge of reservoir sedimentation. The company’s flagship innovation is a proprietary, energy-efficient technology that separates sediment upstream using cyclone manifolds, IoT, AI, and digital twins. Our services include:

Engineering Design & Deployment:

  • Custom design, manufacturing, and installation of multiphase separation systems for aqua ducts, reservoirs, and rivers.
  • Integration with existing infrastructure for hydropower, irrigation, and municipal water systems.

Consulting & Project Management:

  • Feasibility studies, hydrodynamic analysis, and environmental impact assessments.
  • Regulatory compliance and sustainability consulting for water resource projects.

Software & Information Management:

  • Development of digital twins and real-time monitoring platforms.
  • Data analytics, predictive maintenance, and automation for sediment management.

Pilot Projects & Demonstrations:

  • Field testing and validation of new technologies in collaboration with utilities, agencies, and research partners.

Smart Water Management Solutions:

  • Advanced sensor networks, big data analytics, and AI-driven decision support systems for water utilities and hydropower operators.

Environmental Restoration & Climate Adaptation:

  • Solutions for flood risk reduction, habitat restoration, and climate-resilient infrastructure.

Material Recycling & Circular Economy:

  • Technologies for sediment reuse in construction, agriculture, and resource recovery.

Custom Engineering Projects:

  • Tailored projects for small hydropower, river restoration, and high-turbidity water treatment.
